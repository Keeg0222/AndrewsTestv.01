My Son

By: Cara Schmidt

Andrew was my son! He was and is an amazing kid. Andrew was an old soul - everyone knew this. He would hold doors open, take off his hat when eating and most of all he cared. If you had a problem he was there to talk to you. He also loved being outside. From building his ramps at the early age of 3 to clearing the forest and building a bike path last year. He was outside. Andrew also knew how to make a dollar - at a party we had, shortly after moving to Thamesford, (in the pouring rain) he would be the drink deliverer to everyone. If you had to go from one shelter to another he was there with the umbrella too. But this was not free. His services were as long as you left your empties behind! He would clean them all up and return them to the beer store. I love you Andrew and miss you more and more as each day passes. I love your forever I love you always, always my boy you will be! See you again!
