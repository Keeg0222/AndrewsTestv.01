ANDREW'S LEGACY

 

  • THE STORY OF ANDREW

    At the age of 15, Andrew Stoddart was a star athlete, and very active youth, playing sports like football, track and field and of course soccer. He also showed great promise as a quarterback. In an instant our lives changed forever. A young teen taken away from all who loved him. On May 11th 2015 Andrew was playing soccer, a sport he loved, when suddenly collapsed without warning. His coach rushed over to find him without vital signs. She attempted CPR until the fire department arrived with the defibrillator, but it was vain... He was gone. His heart was healthy. It had just... stopped, and no one knows why, But it's happening more and more with young athletes. If there was an AED there close at hand, Andrew might still possibly be with us today.

  • GONE BUT NEVER FORGOTTEN

    Even in death, however, Andrew continues to be an inspiration. We rallied to the cause of getting a AED at every sports field so they will be readily accessible. So that no other parent will have to live the nightmare of coming home without their child. Stickers with #11forever - his jersey number, have popped up in the community, with proceeds going to the cause, and they aren't stopping at their local fields. We want to see them province-wide and we will. Once we've reached that goal - Nationally. Andrew never thought small, so neither will his friends and family. "We need to get these everywhere." said Cara Stoddart, Andrew's mom on the day she laid her first born to rest and we will strive to do so.
